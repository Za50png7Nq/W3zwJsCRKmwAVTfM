    Madhya Pradesh MPPSC State Services Examination 2023

    Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC)

    Short Details of Notification MPPSC SSE Advt No. : 31/2023

    Important Dates

    • Application Begin : 22/09/2023
    • Last Date for Apply Online : 08/11/2023 Upto 12 Noon
    • Pay Exam Fee Last Date : 08/11/2023
    • Last Date Correction : 10/11/2023
    • Exam Date Pre : 17/12/2023
    • Admit Card Available : 08/12/2023
    • Answer Key Available : 20/12/2023
    • Result Available : 19/01/2024
    • Mains Result Available : 30/12/2024

    Application Fee

    • General / Other State : 500/-
    • MP Reserve Category : 250/-
    • Portal Charge : 40/- (Extra)
    • Correction Charge : 50/-
    • Pay the Examination Fee Through Cash at MP Online Kiosk or Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking Fee Mode Only

    Eligibility

    • Age Limit as on 01/01/2024
    • Minimum Age : 21 Years.
    • Maximum Age : 33 Years. for Uniformed Post

    Age relaxation as per rules of MPPSC SSE Advt No. : 31/2023.

    How to Apply

    📢 Important Instructions for Candidates
    ✅ for Applying Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC)

    Go to official Site and Read Instructions and notification for Candidates from official Website before Apply .

    ✅ Read the Notification

    Carefully go through the official notification to understand the recruitment process and requirements.

    ✅ Document Preparation
    • Verify your eligibility and gather essential documents like ID proof, address details, and basic details.
    • Keep scanned copies of necessary documents ready, including your photo, signature, and ID proof.
    ✅ Application Submission

    Before submitting, thoroughly check the preview of your application form and review all fields carefully.

    Take a printout of the final submitted form for future reference.
