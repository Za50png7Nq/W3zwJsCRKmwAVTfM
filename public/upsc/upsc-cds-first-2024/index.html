    UPSC Combined Defence Services Exam First 2024 (CDS I 2024 )

    Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)

    Short Details of Notification UPSC CDA Exam Notice No. 04/2024, CDS-I

    Important Dates

    • Application Begin: 20/12/2023
    • Last Date of Apply Online: 09/01/2024 Till 06
    • Fee Payment Last Date: 09/01/2024
    • Modify / Edit Form: 10-16 January 2024
    • Exam Date: 21/04/2024
    • Admit Card Available: 12/04/2024
    • Result Declared: 09/05/2024
    • Final Result Declared: 21/10/2024
    • Final Marks Available: 13/01/2025

    Application Fee

    • General / OBC : 200/-
    • SC / ST / Female : 0/- (Exempted)
    • Pay the Examination Fee Through Challan or Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card Fee Mode.

    Eligibility

    • Date of Birth :
    • 02/01/2000 to 01/01/2006

    Age relaxation as per rules of UPSC CDA Exam Notice No. 04/2024, CDS-I.

    Vacancy Details

    • Bachelor Degree in Any Stream with Physics, Mathematics in 10+2 Level OR Bachelor Degree in Engineering
